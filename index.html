Register & enable sharing


Set aside monthly sharing quota

Griggi creates an open wifi on top of your password protected wifi to which Griggi users could connect to

If you have 100 GB data & you typically use 50-60 GB every month, you may like to set aside 30GB for wifi sharing.
Sharing is automatically disabled when quota limit for the month is hit



Your router is well protected

Sharing wifi through Griggi is safe. Other Griggi users are on different subnet & could not even access your router admin page.



Built in QoS tools to ensure higher privilege to you

Griggi intelligently throttles other connected Griggi users' bandwidth to ensure you always get acceptable speed
In worst case, you can always force disconnect other Griggi users or disable sharing completely.



Accumulate Karma GBs & seamlessly sell to users who need some

When an Griggi users is using your wifi, his Karma GBs are transferring to you. Any other user who is out of Karma GBs can buy some from you. You can use your Karma GBs to connect to any other Griggi wifi. Its like a virtual currency.



Use your accumulated Karma GBs with others' Griggi wifi

Next time when you work from your favorite cafe, before tethering your phone 3G, check if an Griggi wifi is available & use it.
Its lot faster & cheaper.



Griggi wifis are better than open wifi hotspots

Your Griggi credentials work everywhere & hence you do not need to register or give out your details at every new hotspot.
Griggi is built on top of openwrt, an open source router OS & perform lot better than lot of other hotspot softwares that are closed source, built by a small dev shop & which does not work half the time



See connection speed of an Griggi wifi upfront

You can see the real speed you getting from Griggi wifi prior to connecting & anytime while you are connected.



Griggi wifi are phishing safe

Its easy for a hacker to create an open wifi, name it Griggi & phish your Griggi credentials when you connect & login to them.
You set a secret statement while you register at Griggi which is shown to you when you put in your Griggi username while connecting to an Griggi wifi. A hacker will not know your secret statement.
